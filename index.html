About Pixel by Swaraj

A passion for visual storytelling

At Pixel by Swaraj, we believe that every moment tells a story worth preserving. With years of experience in professional photography and videography, we specialize in capturing life's most significant moments with cinematic quality and artistic vision.

Our approach combines technical expertise with creative storytelling. We pay meticulous attention to detail, from lighting and composition to color grading and post-production. Each project is tailored to reflect your unique vision and personality.

Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or commercial shoot, we deliver professional-grade content that exceeds expectations. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.
